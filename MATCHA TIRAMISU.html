MATCHA TIRAMISU

Prep Time Cook Time Resting Time Total Time
35 minutes 19 minutes 4 hours 4 hrs 45 mins

INGREDIENTS FOR MILKTEA

Name Quantity
Ceylon tea bags 5
water 1 ½ cups
evaporated milk ½ cup

INGREDIENTS FOR CREAMY FILLING

Name Quantity
cream cheese 1 pound
sour cream 2 tablespoons
heavy cream 2 cups
confectioner's (powdered) sugar 1½ cups
vanilla extract 2 teaspoons

INGREDIENTS FOR ASSEMBLING

Name Quantity
ladyfingers 48
matcha powder 3 tablespoons

INSTRUCTIONS FOR MILKTEA

1. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine tea bags and water. Bring to a simmer, then turn off the heat and steep for 10 minutes. Stir in evaporated milk.

2. Pour milk tea into a flat-bottomed container large enough for ladyfingers to lay flat.

INSTRUCTIONS FOR CREAMY FILLING

1. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, beat cream cheese for 2 minutes until soft and fluffy.

2. Add sour cream, heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract, and beat until smooth, about 3 minutes. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate until you assemble the cake.

INSTRUCTIONS FOR ASSEMBLING

1. Working one at a time, quickly dip a ladyfinger into the milk tea, then place it in an 8x10 baking dish. Repeat until you have a single layer of ladyfingers.

2. Spread a third of the creamy filling over the ladyfingers. Sprinkle 1 tablespoon of matcha powder over the creamy filling using a fine sieve.

3. Create a second layer by repeating the above steps.

4. Create a third layer, but don't add matcha powder to the third layer, as it'll get discolored while it sits in the refrigerator.

5. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight. Before serving, dust a final layer of matcha powder on top.
