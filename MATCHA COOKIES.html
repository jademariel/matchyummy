MATCHA COOKIES

Prep Time Cook Time Resting Time
20 minutes 12 minutes 32 minutes

INGREDIENTS

Name Quantity
all-purpose flour 180 g
matcha powder 1 tablespoon
milk powder 1 tablespoon
baking powder ½ teaspoon
baking soda ½ teaspoon
salt ½ teaspoon
unsalted butter 115 g
granulated sugar 150 g
large egg, room temperature 1
vanilla extract 1 teaspoon
white chocolate 100 g

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 350°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Set aside.

2. In a small bowl, stir together the flour, matcha powder, milk powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

3. In a large mixing bowl, use an electric hand mixer to beat softened butter until very creamy. Add sugar and cream together with the mixer until very creamy and fluffy, about 3-4 minutes.

4. Add in the eggs and vanilla extract and mix again until combined.

5. Add the flour mixture into the butter mixture and briefly mix with the hand mixer until almost combined with some flour patches left.

6. Add the chopped chocolate and finish folding the dough together with a silicone spatula until just combined. Don't overmix.

7. Using a 4-tablespoon ice cream scooper, scoop out balls of cookie dough and place them on your baking sheet, leaving about 2" between each cookie for spreading.

8. Bake cookies for 12-14 minutes or until the edges are set but the middle is still very soft. In the last 2 minutes of baking, press a few extra white chocolate chunks on top of each cookie (optional).

9. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for 10 minutes before carefully transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
